    Download (.zip)

Download Instructions

1. Click download button and get the MemeMachine.zip file.

2. Unzip the MemeMachine.zip file — it should be in your downloads folder.

3. Move the MemeMachine folder (located in the Downloads folder) to a directory where you will likely not move it or delete it — e.g. the Documents folder.

4. Open Google Chrome and click on the menu (3 dots stacked) icon in the top right corner, then click on More Tools > Extensions.

5. Turn on Developer Mode on the top right corner by sliding the slider right, or clicking on the slider.

6. Go to the top left hand corner and click the Load Unpacked button, then open the MemeMachine folder.
